I am a HUGE Tarantino movie fan. I hope to one day collect all of his movies. These are some of my favorites.

Pulp Fiction
It is an 2hour action packed, multiscripted movie that shows the audience not all bad guys are bad. Sometimes its just business
Kill Bill Vol 1&2
Kill Bill is the first movie I can remember watching from Tarantino's collection. I loved how he took the Gorge Lucus approch in putting out the movies backwards, first vol 2 and then vol 1. I also really liked Lucy Lus story line and how he chose to put into a comic book style. You really do get that ninja vibe after her life story.
Once Upon a Time in Holloywood
I just went to go see this one this past weekend, so I'll try not to give out too much. BUT I will say it is definetly one of my favorites. I was a little confused for a bit but in true Tarantino fashion he really brought it all home at the end. It is a very well multicripted movie with multipule paradoxes both within the movie and with his characters.
